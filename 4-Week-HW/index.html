Twenty One Pilots

Band Members

  • Tyler Joseph
  • Nick Thomas

This musical duo hails from Columbus, Ohio and their group name is usually stylized with all lowercase letters and zeros. Tyler Joseph performs with his neck and hands blacked out representing his depression and anxiety and he came up with the bands name from a play called "All My Sons" by Arthur Miller about a man that commits suicide after knowingly causing the death of twenty one pilots by sending them faulty parts for profit of his business.
